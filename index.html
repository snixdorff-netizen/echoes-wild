How to Play • ECHOES Playtest

Goal: Explore habitats, listen, record clean calls, and identify species to log them and protect acoustic integrity.

  • WASD or click to move the hiker
  • Hold mouse to grip/charge (fingers hold on) — release to break free and shoot forward with particles
  • Hold L or mouse for directional mic cone (green wedge)
  • Animals call louder and more often when you're close and facing them
  • Press R or use RECORD CLIP while listening
  • Choose the correct species in the identify cards (with spectrogram visual)
  • Use tabs to switch habitats, T or button to change time of day
  • Complete 6/6 in a habitat for a special Field Report you can share

For this playtest: Try to complete at least one full habitat. Use the Feedback button at the end of your session. Feel free to think aloud.
